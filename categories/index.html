Research

Research on deep learning-based spatio-temporal fusion for LST Prof. Qunming Wang, Supervisor
2022 - Present We use sptio-temporal fusion methods to obtain both fine spatial and temporal resolution land surface temperature (LST) data. It should be addressed that challenges exists in coarse spatial resolution data and the differences between the different sensors. Currently, RES-STF, a deep learning-based method was designed by me to overcome the data discrepancy problem. Alternative data VIIRS LST were compared to MODIS LST in fusion and the former performed better.
2021 - 2022 My undergraduate thesis, titled “Research on deep learning-based spatiotemporal fusion methods for land surface temperature”, reviewed performances of existing sptio-temporal fusion algorithms for LST.

Spatiotemporal Evolution and Driving Factors of Urban Technological Innovation Capacity Assoc. Prof. Hong Zhang, Supervisor
2021 - 2022 By using Python crawler programming, we collected and categorised the patent transfer properties then analyzed China’s patent transfer network. We used the complex network and spatial analysis approaches to map the evolution of intercity patent transfer.

Research on Non-Rigid Remote Sensing Image Registration Methods Prof. Yuanxin Ye, Supervisor
2020 - 2021 It was performed under Student Research Training Program (SRTP, for undergraduate), we applied B-spline method to study non-rigid registration algorithms in multimodal remote sensing images.
